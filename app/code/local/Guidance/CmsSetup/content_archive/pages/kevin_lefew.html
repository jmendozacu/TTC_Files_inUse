Kevin LeFew

Senior VP, Technology Services

A recognized leader, Kevin LeFew has been working in the information technology field for more than 25 years and has been at the heart of digital business technology for the past 15. In 2013, Kevin brought his talents to The Great Courses as Senior VP of Technology Services. Responsible for all technology and technical operations, he oversees the creation and execution of strategic and tactical plans used in the development and delivery of services to our customers.

Before joining the company, Kevin was Managing Partner at Digital Fulcrum, LLC, a consulting practice oriented toward the technology needs of digital media clients such as AutoTrader.com, Comcast, Entertainment Arts, and Monster.com. From 2002 to 2010, he served in digital technology leadership for Gannett's media properties, including USATODAY.com. Most recently, he was Vice President of Technology and Operations for Gannett Digital, where he was responsible for all aspects of digital technology across all media properties and a combined budget of more than $20 million.

Throughout his experience, Kevin worked with clients such as NASA, NeuStar Domain Services, and the Naval Air Systems Command, and served on executive steering committees for several corporations.

Kevin holds an M.B.A. in Information Systems Management, a bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, and a Federal CIO Certificate through the Advanced Management Program at the National Defense University, Information Resources Management College.

Kevin is an avid golfer, and his favorite Great Course to date is Fundamentals of Photography.
