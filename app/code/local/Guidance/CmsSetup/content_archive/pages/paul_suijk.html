Paul Suijk

President

An innovative and strategically focused executive, Paul Suijk was named President of The Great Courses in May 2013. Previously, he served as Chief Financial Officer & Secretary for more than five years, directing the financial management of the company as well as the distribution and fulfillment operation, the call center, and the financial planning and analysis department.

Before joining The Great Courses, Paul served as CFO of Cadmus Communications, a content processing and print company. At Cadmus, he developed its strategic plan, positioned the company for consolidation, and negotiated a successful sale to Cenveo, creating the third-largest publicly traded print company in the United States.

Paul also served as CFO at Comdial Corporation, a telecommunications company, and held senior financial management positions at Danka Business Systems, James River Corporation, and Royal Dutch Shell. He has worked in operations throughout the world, including Asia, Europe, and North America.

Throughout his career Paul provided key strategic leadership for each of the firms he worked for and specialized in developing joint ventures, license agreements, and other partnerships. He holds a master's degree in Finance and Investments and a bachelor's degree in International Business from George Washington University.

Paul's favorite Great Courses are The Art of Storytelling, How to Listen to and Understand Great Music, and the Great Masters series.

Paul is married to Julie and has four children. He enjoys sailing, spending time outdoors, and relaxing in the Virgin Islands.
