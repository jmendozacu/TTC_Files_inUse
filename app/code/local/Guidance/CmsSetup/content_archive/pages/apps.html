Take your Great Courses everywhere you go!

The Great Courses has always taken the learning experience out of the classroom and into your home. Now, with our mobile apps, take your favorite professor with you while you exercise, run errands, or travel.

Our Apps

The Great Courses App for iPhone and iPad

The Great Courses is a free download exclusively for lifelong learners with The Great Courses. This application allows you to stream your digital courses anywhere you want to be: at work, at home, or on the road.

The Great Courses application allows you to:
  • View any courses you’ve already purchased
  • Easily track which courses and lectures you’ve completed
  • Enhance your learning experience with the included Course Guidebook (PDF) The Great Courses: igniting people's passion for lifelong learning by offering great courses taught by great professors.
 

The Great Courses App for Android

Not an Apple person? No problem! The Great Courses app is also available for Android, so you can enjoy your favorite lectures on your Android smartphone or Android tablet. Requires OS 2.3.3 or higher.

 

The Great Courses App for Kindle Fire

Take advantage of the beautiful color display of the Kindle Fire with The Great Courses app, and stream video or audio courses wherever you have an internet connection. Requires OS 2.3.3 or higher.
