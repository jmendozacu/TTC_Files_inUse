Bruce G. Willis

Senior VP, Finance & Operations

Bruce Willis brings more than 25 years of experience in finance, information technology, procurement, and distribution to The Great Courses. A seasoned project management leader, he is responsible for overseeing all of the company's extensive accounting, tax, and treasury services. He also has oversight of the product fulfillment operation, outsourced manufacturing, and the customer care teams. Bruce skillfully leads a multifunctional team of professionals who are all dedicated to providing an efficient and superior customer experience.

Before joining the company in 2009, he spent 16 years in various leadership roles with Cadmus Communications, a printing and publishing services provider. He also spent a combined nine years working with the accounting firms of Arthur Andersen and Coopers & Lybrand.

Bruce earned his bachelor's degree from the University of Richmond. In his spare time, Bruce enjoys cheering for local sports teams, playing tennis, reading, and spending time with his wife and son. Among his favorite Great Courses are The Science of Natural Healing, Physiology and Fitness, and London: A Short History of the Greatest City in the Western World.
