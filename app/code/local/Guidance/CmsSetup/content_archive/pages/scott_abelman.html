Scott Ableman

Chief Marketing Officer

Shortly after joining The Great Courses in 2012 as Executive Vice President of Ecommerce, Scott Ableman was named as the company's first-ever Chief Marketing Officer. In this expansive role, he leads the company's investments in the online, mobile, and digital experience.

Scott has extensive experience in ecommerce, brand marketing, product development, and customer experience. He previously served as Chief Marketing Officer at Simplexity, where he built the Wirefly.com brand and helped the company become the Internet's leading retailer of cell phones and wireless services.

As Chief Marketing Officer at Winstar Communications, Scott built a nationwide marketing organization to offer telecommunications and Internet services to small businesses. He also held several management positions in consumer marketing and product development at Network Solutions and MCI Communications.

Scott earned his M.B.A. from the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University and his bachelor's degree in Consumer Behavior from the University of Pennsylvania.

In his spare time, Scott enjoys photography, expedition travel, and rooting for the Washington Nationals. His ever-expanding list of favorite Great Courses includes Foundations of Western Civilization, Biology and Human Behavior, Fundamentals of Photography, and The Art of Critical Decision Making. He lives in McLean, VA, with his wife and two children.
