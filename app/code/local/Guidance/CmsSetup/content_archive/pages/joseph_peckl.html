Joseph Peckl

VP, Marketing

Joseph Peckl joined The Great Courses as Vice President of Marketing in 2007, bringing more than 20 years of experience in direct marketing, product development, and ecommerce at leading multinational corporations. In his role, he has championed marketing initiatives that have significantly increased revenue and profits and have dramatically expanded the company's customer base into international markets.

Before joining The Great Courses, Joseph worked for some of the country's largest and most successful direct marketing and media companies, including PRIMEDIA, where he was Vice President, Group Publisher for the organization's history magazines and websites, which included publications devoted to the Civil War and aviation, military, and British history. He also spent 14 years in various senior positions at Time Warner, Inc., both domestically and in London, where he was Senior Vice President of eCommerce for Time Life and Managing Director of Book of the Month Club, UK.

Joseph is a graduate of Carnegie Mellon University and holds a B.S. in Applied Mathematics and Operations Research and Management Sciences.

Away from the office, he is an avid golfer who enjoys spending time with his wife and family, which includes a son, a daughter, and two Labrador retrievers.
