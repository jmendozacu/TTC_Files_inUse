Our Story
It all started with a Harvard Law student…

Thomas M. Rollins, the founder of The Great Courses, was a law student at Harvard University facing an important exam on the U.S. Federal Rules of Evidence ­—an exam for which he wasn’t prepared.


Dreading the notoriously boring subject but knowing his success depended on understanding the material, Rollins obtained videotapes of 10 lectures by a professor from another school who was noted for being insightful, thorough, and witty.


Rollins played the videotaped lectures nearly nonstop, and a few days later he got an “A” on his exam. Rollins never forgot the unique power of recorded lectures by a great teacher, the way that a bright mind could ignite a passion for lifelong learning.


In 1990, he founded The Great Courses to share that unforgettable experience with the rest of the world.
